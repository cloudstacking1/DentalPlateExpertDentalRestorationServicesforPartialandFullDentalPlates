What Are Dental Plates and How Do They Work?

Dental plates, often referred to as denture plates, are removable dental appliances that are used to replace missing teeth. At Dental Plate, we offer both partial and full dental plates to meet the needs of our patients. These plates are custom-made to fit your mouth and are designed to restore both the function and appearance of your smile.

Partial Dental Plates are ideal for patients who still have some of their natural teeth remaining. These plates fill the gaps created by missing teeth and help prevent the remaining natural teeth from shifting. Full Dental Plates are used for patients who have lost all of their natural teeth. They are designed to provide a complete set of teeth and restore full functionality to your mouth.
